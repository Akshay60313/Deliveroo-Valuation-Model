 Strategic Valuation: Deliveroo Plc (M&A Fairness Opinion)

**Project Overview
This project involves building a comprehensive financial model to assess the fairness of DoorDash’s cash offer of **£1.80 per share** for Deliveroo Plc.

**Key Tools & Techniques**
* **Financial Modeling:** 6-Year 3-Statement Forecast (P&L, Balance Sheet drivers, Cash Flow).
* **DCF Analysis:** Intrinsic valuation using a mid-year convention and WACC sensitivity.
* **Scenario Modeling:** "Unit Economics" sensitivity analysis on Rider Costs (£4.55 vs £5.00).
* **Visuals:** Football Field Valuation Chart & Dynamic Dashboards.

**Summary of Findings**
* **Intrinsic Value:** Calculated a base case share price of **£2.21**.
* **Market Value:** Comparable Company Analysis (EV/EBITDA) implied **£1.68**.
* **Conclusion:** The DoorDash offer of £1.80 represents a fair market entry but a **~20% discount** to the company's long-term intrinsic potential.
